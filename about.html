About Me and this Project


I am a senior at the University of Alabama. This website is my Blount Scholars Program Worldviews project.

I wanted to make something that allowed me to use my cyber security knowledge and couple it with my love of education.

In this project, I researched the ways that the youth of today learn best and designed a website to give them the opportunity to learn cyber security skills and facts.


The primary theory that I am using for this project is Risk Awareness in society which comes from Ulrich Beck's World at Risk

The idea behind this theory is that one is only aware of the risks that their society is aware of. So if one's society, or generation, is not well-versed in the dangers of cyberspace, they are more likely to fall victim to scams.
