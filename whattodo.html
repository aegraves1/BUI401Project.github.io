When you get a phishing scheme, report it

If your credentials are stolen, change your passwords. Make sure you use strong passwords that you can remember. It is a balance between complexity and ease of memory.

Set up 2-Factor Authentication

Having 2-Factor Authentication on your account will ensure that you know if someone else is trying to access your account.

Sign out of all your accounts

If your device is stolen, you will want to sign out of everything so that the thief does not get access to anything else.
